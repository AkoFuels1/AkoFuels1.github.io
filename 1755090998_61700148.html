Frequently Asked Questions

How do I update my payment method?

You can update your payment method by navigating to 'Settings' > 'Payment Details' and selecting the 'Edit' option next to your current payment method.

What should I do if my payment failed?

First, check your internet connection and ensure your card details are correct. If the issue persists, contact your bank or try an alternative payment method.

Is my personal information secure?

Yes, we use industry-standard encryption and security protocols to protect your personal and payment information. Your data privacy is our top priority.
